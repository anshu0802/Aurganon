About Aurganon

Aurganon is a national level technical symposium featurng a myriad of events aiming to enrich young minds with fine technical knowledge. It provides a platform for the participants to experience one of the most beautiful and exuberant amalgamation of technology and fun.
Be it competitive coding or a simple game of treasure hunt, Aurganon has it all. While the wide range of events and competitions being conducted are sure to cater to everyone's taste, the creative themes selected make them even more fascinating and engaging.
